KyoTore Support

KyoTore is a workout logging app for recording exercises, body data, and training progress.

Contact

For support requests, purchase restore questions, bug reports, or privacy questions, contact: KyoToreee@gmail.com

Purchases

If you purchased KyoTore Pro, use the Restore purchases button in the app settings screen to restore your entitlement on the same Apple ID.

Local Data

Workout records and body records are stored locally on your device. If you delete the app or delete local data in the app, those local records are removed from the device.
