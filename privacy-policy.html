KyoTore Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-15

KyoTore is a workout logging app for recording exercises, body data, and training plans.

Data Stored by the App

KyoTore stores workout records, body records, exercise settings, profile settings, pinned Today's workout items, and app preferences locally on your device.

KyoTore does not currently operate its own cloud database or server for these records. Workout records, body records, and app preferences are not sent to KyoTore servers.

Advertising

Some versions of KyoTore may use Google AdMob to display ads for free users. When ads are enabled, Google and its partners may collect device identifiers, advertising identifiers, usage information, and other data needed to deliver, personalize, and measure ads.

For details, see Google's policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • AdMob privacy and policy information

If a KyoTore version does not include advertising SDKs, that version does not collect advertising data through AdMob.

Payments

Some versions of KyoTore may offer KyoTore Pro or other paid features.

  • On Android, purchases are handled by Google Play Billing.
  • On iOS, purchases are handled by Apple's App Store payment system.

Payment information is processed by the applicable app store provider. KyoTore stores only the local entitlement state needed to unlock paid features, unless a future version explicitly introduces account-based sync.

Data Deletion

You can delete local app data from within the app where available, or by deleting KyoTore from your device. On Android, you can also clear KyoTore's storage from system settings.

Deleting local app data removes local workout records, body records, exercise settings, pinned Today's workout items, and app preferences stored on the device. Purchase history and refunds are managed by the applicable app store provider.
