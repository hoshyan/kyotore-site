KyoTore Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-08

KyoTore is a workout logging app for recording exercises, body data, and training plans.

Data Stored by the App

KyoTore stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Workout records such as exercise name, weight, reps, RPE, distance, time, calories, and notes.
  • Body records such as body weight, body fat percentage, and BMI.
  • App settings such as suggestion preferences, pinned Today's workout items, custom exercises, and usage counters.
  • Pro entitlement status used to unlock paid features.

KyoTore does not currently operate its own cloud database or server for these records.

Advertising

KyoTore uses Google AdMob to display ads for free users. Google and its partners may collect device identifiers, ad identifiers, usage information, and other data needed to deliver and measure ads.

For details, see Google's policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • AdMob privacy and policy information

Pro users can hide ads inside the app.

Payments

KyoTore uses Google Play Billing for the KyoTore Pro subscription. Payment information is processed by Google Play. KyoTore stores only the local entitlement state needed to unlock Pro features.

Data Deletion

You can delete local app data from the app settings screen. This removes local workout records, body records, suggestion settings, custom exercises, Today's workout pins, and usage counters. Pro purchase status is preserved and can be restored through Google Play Billing.

You can also delete app data from Android system settings by clearing KyoTore's storage.
